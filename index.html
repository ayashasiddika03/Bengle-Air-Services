About Us

Welcome to Bengal Air Services

Bengal Air Services is your ultimate travel partner, dedicated to serving you with integrity and expertise since 1984.

Our team specializes in seamless air ticket booking, efficient visa processing, and top-notch hotel reservations. We take pride in offering personalized service and are committed to turning your travel dreams into reality. Whether you seek spiritual fulfilment through Hajj and Umrah or thrilling adventures through our tour packages, we are here to support you every step of the way.

Embark on a journey with us, celebrating the beauty of exploration and creating unforgettable memories. Let us be your guide on a journey that celebrates diversity and connection, ensuring that every journey exceeds your expectations and fulfils your desires. Experience the difference with Bengal Air Services – where your travel needs are our top priority.
